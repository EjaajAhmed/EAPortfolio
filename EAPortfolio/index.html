About Me

In 2021, I began my education in Computer Science. Since then I have grown substantially.

Not physically (I’ve grown an inch since then), but mentally. I have made great strides not only in the realm of computer science but also in other disciplines.

As a programmer, my background has always been in Java. When I was in grade 5, I used to try completing the Java course on Khan Academy (I didn’t come close), so six years later in high school, I was thrilled to have an opportunity to work with Java. It became my foundation and my window into the tech world.

That was until I took a university course in web development. HTML, CSS, and JavaScript blew my mind. The fact that I could create a website was surreal, and discovering that Java and JavaScript were different languages almost gave me an aneurysm. From there, I explored C, Python, RISC-V, and SQL, along with more advanced Java concepts like object-oriented programming (OOP).

Besides programming I am a huge fan of sports. The determination and will to win of humans has always been the most amazing thing to me. I also enjoy reading, biking, chess and creating stories.
